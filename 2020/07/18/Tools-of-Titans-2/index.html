Share

Make your bed

  • Make up your bed every morning no matter what, even when you’re staying in a hotel.
  • Clean environment will make you have a sense of satification.
  • Spend 1 minitue to makeup your bed, you will see the difference.

Meditate

  • Miditate for 10-20 minutes each day
  • Have a daily mindfullness practice
  • Two Apps recommended to better practice meditation: HEADSPACE & Calm
  • More details regarding how to meditate can be found at How to Mediate

Turn into the peak state

  • Cold Shower to refresh your mind
  • Breathing Exercise: The ratio for “Inhale”:”Hold Breath”:”Exhale” should be 1:4:2, for example, take 4 seconds to inhale, 16 seconds to hold your breath and 8 seconds to exhale.
  • Do this few minitues
  • Do Short time of body exercises for 5-10 times, it can be pushups , deep squat , jump in place or other exercises

5-10 Minutes Journal

  • Recommend 2 types of journaling
    • The problems you’re facing
    • Prioritizing and gratitude
      • 3 important thing you have to finish today
      • 3 things you’re grateful for
