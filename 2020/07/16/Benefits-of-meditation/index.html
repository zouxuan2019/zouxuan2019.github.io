Top 5 Benefits of meditation

Share

Increase ability to endure pain

  • Reduce pain 40%
  • Reduce comfortable 57%
  • Meditation is better than painkiller

Upgrade your brain

  • 8 Weeks of meditation improve 3 brain areas
    • Hippocampus & Perfrontal cortex which incharge of ability to learn & memory
    • Posterior Cingulate Cortex which control of mind & thinking
    • Temporop arietal junction which incharge of compassion & empathy
  • Not only increate the brain size of important areas, it also reduce amygdala cell which incharge of fear anxiety & stress
  • Faster info processing
  • Faster decision making
  • Faster memory forming & focus

Reduce bad emotion

  • Reduce depression, stress & anxiety
  • Effective last above 6 months
  • Withnot sign of depression
  • Reduce overthinking & loss of confident
  • Increase in happiness
  • Improve performance on tests on cognitive skill
  • Some even 10x better
  • Better performance in tasks with deadline pressure

Super Healthy

  • Telomeres, meditation slow down the rate of cellular aging
  • Reduce in blood pressure
  • Reduce in social pressure
  • Meditation can reduce alzheimers

Super Happy

  • Improve a person’s ability to read the facial expressions of others
  • Be more present & aware of the surrounding
