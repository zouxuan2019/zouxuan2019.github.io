Happiness Bottle - Record great things

  • Anytime something really cool happens in a day, excited or joyful, write it down on a note and put in inside the bottle.
  • When you feel depressed, take a look on the great memories.
  • Great things example: watched a fantastic movie, read a great book, met an old friend…
  • If you really don’t know what to write, you can simply put “I’m still alive today”

Randomly wishing people

  • 10-second exercise, pick 2 random people and wish them to be happy
  • Practice the exercise at any time, any place
  • Help other people like “Buying a coffee for the people stand behind you” ,”Praise strangers” or “Do voluntary work”…
