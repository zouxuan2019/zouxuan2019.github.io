3 Life Advices that can get you out of Dilemma

No Need to Wait

  • If you have a 10-Year plan to achieve your plan, why can’t you do this in 6 months?
  • If someone point a gun at your head, force you to complete your plan in 6 months, what would you do?
  • Break out of the box, break the rules
  • If you want to get something you’ve never had before, then you must do something that you’ve never done before.
  • Not everybody should do the same thing

Escape from comfort zone

  • Fear-Rehearsing, Exposure to the “Bad”
  • Lucius Annaeus Seneca tried 3-14 days simulate losing all money, he only did a few things
    • Sleeping in sleeping bag whether indoor or outside
    • Wearing cheap white shirt & Single pair of Jeans
    • Couchsurfing.com or similar services
    • Eating only instant oat or rice
    • Drinking only water and cheap instant coffee
    • Online only at libraries
  • You will happier after done all those things
  • 明明自由是可能的，但我们却让自己陷入一成不变但陈旧模式中勉强的过日子…

Use what you have

  • Robert Rodriguez: There’s a freedom in limitation
  • 有时候缺乏资源，才是取得伟大成就的关键因素。如果你真的想要做一件事情，你永远都会找到方法的。
  • Take action!!!
