If there are a lot of outliers in your data, you may want to explore more on below 3 regularized regressions. They restrict the actual movement of the line, so that it won’t be badly impacted by outliers

Summary When should I use Lasso, Ridge or Elastic Net?

  • Ridge regression : can’t zero out coefficients;You either end up including all the coefficients in the model, or none of them.
  • LASSO:Does both parameter shrinkage and variable selection automatic?
  • If some of your covariates are highly correlated, you may want to look at the Elastic Net instead of the LASSO.
